TL;DR: 在LHS 1903红矮星系统中发现了四颗行星，它们跨越了半径谷，为研究行星形成和大气演化提供了独特案例。

Motivation: 研究小行星半径分布中的"半径谷"现象，探索其成因（恒星加热、内部加热或初始包层缺失），并通过单个多行星系统验证相关理论。

Method: 使用凌星测光法和径向速度测量法探测并表征LHS 1903红矮星周围的四颗行星，分析其轨道周期、半径和密度特征。

Result: 发现四颗行星轨道周期在2.2-29.3天之间，跨越半径谷：LHS 1903 b为岩石行星，c和d有扩展大气层，最远的e行星无气态包层，表明其形成于气体耗尽物质。

Conclusion: LHS 1903系统为半径谷现象提供了关键证据，支持大气剥离理论，并显示行星形成环境的气体丰度差异会影响最终行星类型。

Abstract: Small exoplanet radii show two populations, referred to as super-Earths and sub-Neptunes, separated by a gap known as the radius valley. This may be produced by the removal of atmospheres due to stellar or internal heating, or lack of an initial envelope. We us transit photometry and radial velocity measurements to detect and characterize four planets orbiting LHS 1903, a red dwarf (M-dwarf) star in the Milky Way's thick disk. The planets have orbital periods between 2.2 and 29.3 days, and span the radius valley within a single planetary system. The derived densities indicate that LHS 1903 b is rocky, while LHS 1903 c and LHS 1903 d have extended atmospheres. Although the most distant planet from the host star, LHS 1903 e, has no gaseous envelope, indicating it formed from gas-depleted material.
