### [1] [The Low-$α$ Splash Population in the Milky Way](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.16050)
*Lais Borbolato,João A. S. Amarante,Hélio D. Perottoni,Silvia Rossi,Victor P. Debattista,Zhao-Yu Li,Nathan Deg,Tigran Khachaturyants*

Main category: astro-ph.GA

TL;DR: 本文研究了银河系原位晕中低α元素的Splash族群，发现其由早期星暴团块而非单一并合事件加热形成，对应被动力学加热的老年薄盘恒星。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 近期发现存在老年薄盘星族，这促使研究者探究低α元素的Splash族群是否存在，以验证任何能够加热盘星的机制都应同时影响高α和低α的老年星族这一猜想。

Method: 利用APOGEE DR17光谱巡天数据识别具有晕状运动学特征的金属丰度低α恒星，并借助GASTRO模拟套件对比分析星暴团块散射与主并合事件两种加热机制的效果。

Result: 只有包含早期星暴团块的银河系模型能够同时产生高α和低α的Splash族群，而仅包含并合事件、缺乏早期团块阶段的模型则无法产生这些族群。

Conclusion: 低αSplash是老年薄盘星族的一个子集，其动力学加热机制与形成高αSplash的机制相同，即早期星暴团块的散射作用。

Abstract: The Milky Way in-situ halo, also known as the Splash, consists of old (Age $>$ 10 Gyr), metal-rich ([Fe/H] $> -0.7$), high-$α$ stars, i.e., thick disk-like chemistry, on halo-like orbits (eccentricity > 0.6). Its origin is linked to stars formed in the disk and dynamically heated by either internal or external agents. In this work, we investigate its low-$α$ counterpart, the low-$α$ Splash, motivated by recent findings of an old thin disk population. We conjecture that any mechanism capable of heating disk stars should affect both of present-day high- and low-$α$ old populations. Using data from the APOGEE DR17 spectroscopic catalog, we identify metal-rich low-$α$ stars with halo-like kinematics similar to those of the classical high-$α$ Splash. We investigate their possible heating mechanisms using the GASTRO suite of simulations, which allows us to explore the effects of star-forming clumps as well as a major merger in the proto-disk of a Milky Way analog galaxy. Our main results show that only clumpy Milky Way models are able to produce Splash populations through scattering by clumps in the early Galaxy, including the low-$α$ counterpart, whereas the model including only the merger and without an early clumpy phase fails to produce these populations. In the models, the low-$α$ Splash corresponds to a subset of the old thin disk that was dynamically heated by the same mechanism responsible for the formation of the high-$α$ Splash.

### [2] [Kinematic hints of a nuclear bar in the Milky Way](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.16105)
*Karl Fiteni,Mattia C. Sormani,Victor P. Debattista,Francisco Nogueras-Lara,Rainer Schödel,Jason L. Sanders,Mathias Schultheis,Xingchen Li,Arianna Vasini,Zi-Xuan Feng,Marco Donati*

Main category: astro-ph.GA

TL;DR: 通过分析恒星运动学数据，发现了银河系核星盘内可能存在核棒结构的首个运动学证据。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 银河系内部存在一个扁平核星盘（NSD），但其是否为纯轴对称结构或包含核棒尚不清楚，本文旨在使用运动学诊断方法检验核棒的存在。

Method: 结合KMOS NSD巡天的视向速度和VIRAC2的自行数据，构建速度椭圆，测量顶点偏差l_v和各向异性β，并严格控制大尺度棒星的污染。

Result: 主样本得到显著的负顶点偏差l_v = -54.8°和中等各向异性β=0.16；最内场样本信号更强，l_v = -64.3°，β=0.38。速度弥散最大方向沿银经方向，与大尺度棒主导样本相反。

Conclusion: 运动学信号与轴对称核星盘不符，但与取向约60°-75°的核棒预期一致，提供了银河系可能存在核棒的首个运动学证据。

Abstract: The Milky Way hosts a flattened nuclear stellar disc (NSD) that dominates the gravitational potential in the inner few hundred parsecs. Whether the NSD is purely axisymmetric or contains a nuclear bar remains an open question. We test for the presence of a nuclear bar using kinematic diagnostics by combining line-of-sight velocities from the KMOS NSD survey with proper motions from VIRAC2 to construct the $ (v_\ell, v_\mathrm{los}) $ velocity ellipse. After applying strict quality cuts to minimise contamination from large-scale bar stars, we measure the vertex deviation $ l_v $ and anisotropy $ β$ for several subsamples. For our primary sample ($ |\ell| < 0.9^\circ $, $ -0.4^\circ < b < 0.25^\circ $, $ \mathrm{[Fe/H]} > -0.3 $), we find a significant negative vertex deviation $ l_v = -54.8^{+13.1}_{-14.8}\,^\circ $ with moderate anisotropy $ β= 0.16^{+0.08}_{-0.05} $. A subsample restricted to the innermost four fields yields an even stronger signal with $ l_v = -64.3^{+12.1}_{-12.2}\,^\circ $ and $ β= 0.38^{+0.12}_{-0.07} $. The direction of maximum velocity dispersion is oriented along Galactic longitude, opposite to that observed in large-scale bar-dominated samples. These signatures are robust against extinction-driven incompleteness, primary-bar contamination, and the choice of metallicity threshold. They are inconsistent with an axisymmetric NSD or one oriented orthogonally to the primary bar, but match expectations for a nuclear bar oriented at $ α\approx 60^\circ $-$75^\circ$ to the Sun-Galactic-Centre line with its near side pointing toward positive Galactic longitude. While definitive confirmation awaits larger and more precise samples from upcoming surveys, our results provide the first kinematic indication of a possible nuclear bar in the Milky Way.

### [3] [No Stream Left Unscathed: The imprint of a host galaxy](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.16200)
*Arpit Arora,Peter S. Ferguson,Jacob Nibauer,Nora Shipp,Videep Reddy,Eugene Vasiliev,Jack Kohm,Laurella C. Marin,Adrian M. Price-Whelan,Denis Erkal,Sarah Pearson,Andrew Wetzel,Jeremy Bailin,Robert Feldmann*

Main category: astro-ph.GA

TL;DR: 该研究通过模拟约15,000条银河系星流（仅考虑宿主势场，排除暗物质子晕等小尺度扰动），发现约3/4的星流会产生复杂结构（如缝隙、扭结等），且仅约70条流完全平滑。近心距离是形态的主要决定因素，许多观测特征（如GD-1 spur）可在无子晕条件下再现，表明未来巡天需以此基线来提取暗物质子晕约束。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 观测到的银河系星流展现出多样的复杂特征（缝隙、扭结、茧状结构等），传统上多归因于暗物质子晕的撞击。但宿主星系势场本身（如盘、晕的演化）对星流形态的贡献尚不明确，这限制了将星流作为暗物质子晕探针的可靠性。

Method: 基于FIRE-2宇宙学模拟中的四个银河系质量级暗物质晕，采用基函数展开势场（包含演化的盘、晕和大尺度结构，但排除暗物质子晕和巨分子云等小尺度扰动），模拟了约15,000条球状星团瓦解产生的星流演化。

Result: 约75%的星流在仅宿主势场作用下就发展出复杂形态；最平滑的星流也存在10-25%的宽度变化以及约2°尺度的过密/空隙（与子晕预测的1-5°范围重叠）；近心距是主要预测因子，约15 kpc为平滑与扰动流的分界线；仅约70/15,000条流在所有尺度上无可检测的摆动；GD-1 spur和ATLAS-Aliqa Uma扭结等观测特征可在无子晕条件下再现。

Conclusion: 星流中许多复杂结构可能来自宿主势场而非暗物质子晕，未来LSST、Euclid和Roman等巡天项目在利用数百条星流反演暗物质子晕约束时，必须将本工作建立的"无小尺度扰动"基线作为参考基准。

Abstract: Stellar streams from disrupted globular clusters are excellent probes of dark matter (DM) subhalos. Observed Milky Way streams display a remarkable diversity of features: spurs, gaps, kinks, cocoons, and density variations, many attributed to subhalo encounters. But how much of this diversity arises from the host itself? We simulate $\sim$15,000 globular cluster streams across four Milky Way-mass halos from the FIRE-2 cosmological simulations, evolved in basis function expansion potentials capturing the evolving disk, halo, and large-scale structure while excluding small-scale perturbers such as DM subhalos and giant molecular clouds. We find that roughly three quarters of streams develop complex features from the host potential, such as spurs, kinks, and cocoon-like envelopes. Even the smoothest streams exhibit 10--25\% width variation along their track and host overdensities and gaps at scales of ${\sim}2^\circ$, squarely in the $1^\circ$--$5^\circ$ range predicted for subhalo-induced gaps. Pericentric distance is the primary predictor of stream morphology, with ${\sim}15$ kpc separating smooth from disturbed streams and circular orbits beyond $\sim$20 kpc producing the smoothest streams. Only $\sim$70 out of $\sim$15,000 streams are free of detectable wiggles in the track at any scale. Analogs to observed features, such as the GD-1 spur and the ATLAS--Aliqa Uma kink, emerge even without the presence of subhalos. As next-generation surveys (LSST, Euclid, and Roman) resolve stream structure across hundreds of streams, the baseline established here, streams evolved without small-scale perturbers, becomes essential for extracting DM substructure constraints.

</details>

### [4] [Charting circumstellar chemistry of carbon-rich asymptotic giant branch stars. III. SiO and SiS abundances](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.15321)
*R. Unnikrishnan,E. De Beck,L. -Å. Nyman,H. Olofsson,W. H. T. Vlemmings,M. Maercker,M. Van de Sande,T. J. Millar,T. Danilovich,M. Andriantsaralaza,S. B. Charnley,M. G. Rawlings*

Main category: astro-ph.SR

TL;DR: 本文利用ALMA空间分辨成像和单口径观测数据，通过1D非LTE辐射传输模型，对五颗碳星（除AFGL 3068外）的SiO、SiS及其同位素体丰度进行了精确估算，发现SiO丰度在IRC+10216以外的碳星中约高5倍，且SiS的光解离速率可能被低估。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 目前对富碳AGB星化学的认识主要基于单一天体IRC+10216，且现有丰度估计方法（单口径或干涉成像）存在局限，需要对多个碳星样本进行更精确的分子丰度测量。

Method: 利用1D非局部热力学平衡（非LTE）辐射传输（RT）模型，结合ALMA空间分辨成像和单口径观测的形态与激发信息，推导丰度，并与化学模型结果对比。

Result: SiS峰值丰度在各源中非常接近（2.0×10⁻⁶–4.7×10⁻⁶），但IRC+10216之外碳星的SiO峰值丰度约高出5倍；SiO和SiS的e-folding半径随气体密度增大而增加；化学模型能较好重现SiO丰度分布，但高估了SiS的e-folding半径。

Conclusion: 1D非LTE RT模型在空间分辨观测约束下能有效推导碳星丰度，但存在固有局限；当前化学模型中SiS的光解离速率可能被低估。

Abstract: The present understanding of C-rich AGB chemistry largely relies on observations of the archetypal carbon star IRC+10216. Current molecular abundance estimates for carbon stars are based either on single-dish spectra sampling a range of excitation conditions, or on interferometric mapping of a few lines. We aim to estimate the circumstellar abundances of SiO, SiS, and their most abundant isotopologues ($^{29}$SiO, $^{30}$SiO, $^{29}$SiS, $^{30}$SiS, and Si$^{34}$S) for a sample of five carbon stars. We derived molecular abundances using detailed 1D non-local thermodynamic equilibrium (non-LTE) radiative transfer (RT) modelling, constrained by both morphological and excitation information obtained from spatially resolved ALMA maps and single-dish observations. We further compared the derived abundances to chemical modelling results. We obtain good fits to the SiO and SiS line profiles, and derived well-constrained abundance profiles and reliable isotopic ratios for all sources except AFGL 3068. While the SiS peak abundances are very similar across the sample (2.0$\times$10$^{-6}-4.7\times$10$^{-6}$), we find that the SiO peak abundances of the rest of the stars are a factor of $\sim$5 larger than that of IRC+10216. The $e$-folding radii ($R_\mathrm{e}$) are in the range 1.3$\times$10$^{16}-7.0\times$10$^{16}$ cm for SiO and 6.0$\times$10$^{15}-1.0\times$10$^{17}$ cm for SiS. The $R_\mathrm{e}$ increases with gas density for both SiO and SiS. Chemical models reproduce the derived SiO abundance profiles well, while over-predicting the SiS $R_\mathrm{e}$ values. Our models highlight the necessity of having spatially resolved observations across a broad range of excitation conditions, while also making evident the limitations inherent in 1D RT modelling using simplified (circum)stellar models. We find that the currently assumed SiS photodissociation rate in chemical models is underestimated.

</details>
