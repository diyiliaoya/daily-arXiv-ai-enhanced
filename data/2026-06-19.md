We test our method on the complex microlensing event OGLE-2017-BLG-0114, which exhibits signatures that can be traced to the complexity of the source system. We successfully constrained the microlensing models, achieving improvements in the Einstein ring radius estimates by up to an order of magnitude in the case of binary source models.
