This lightweight (0.45 MB) and scalable framework provides an interpretable and efficient solution for large-scale surveys such as Euclid and LSST, advancing data-driven studies of galaxy evolution.

(2) Retrieval studies revealed that restricted wavelength ranges (e.g., 0.4 - 0.7 microns), such as may be used during initial survey strategies, are not sufficient to deconvolve the biopigment features from the abiotic background.

### [32] [LUMOS : Linear programming Utility for Multi-messenger Optical Scheduling](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.08912)
*Yogesh P. Wagh,Michael W. Coughlin,Leo P. Singer,Varun Bhalerao*

Main category: astro-ph.IM

TL;DR: LUMOS是一个基于混合整数线性规划的调度系统，用于优化引力波事件的光学后续观测，相比现有方法显著提高了累积概率覆盖率。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: LIGO-Virgo-KAGRA探测到的引力波事件为多信使天体物理学开辟了新途径，但由于定位不确定性大，电磁对应体仍然难以发现。像ZTF这样的宽视场光学巡天在后续观测中起关键作用，但需要高效的调度策略。

Method: 提出LUMOS方法，采用混合整数线性规划（MILP）方法，通过最大覆盖问题选择观测天区，并调度观测以最大化累积概率，同时满足可观测性约束。

Result: 使用O4期间的1199个引力波事件进行测试，LUMOS调度器相比gwemopt方法，平均累积概率提高了84.7%，在几乎所有情况下都表现更好。

Conclusion: 虽然专为ZTF设计，但LUMOS框架与空间任务的M4OPT工具包类似，突显了基于MILP的调度方法在地面和空间后续观测中的广泛适用性。

Abstract: The detection of gravitational-wave events by LIGO-Virgo-KAGRA has opened new avenues for multi-messenger astrophysics; however, electromagnetic counterparts remain elusive due to large localization uncertainties. Wide-field optical surveys like the Zwicky Transient Facility (ZTF) play a crucial role in follow-up, but efficient scheduling is essential. In this work, we present LUMOS, a Mixed Integer Linear Programming (MILP) approach that selects fields via a maximum coverage problem and schedules observations to maximize cumulative probability while respecting observability constraints. Using 1199 GW events from O4, we compare the LUMOS scheduler with gwemopt, showing an 84.7 percent higher mean cumulative probability and better performance in nearly all cases. While designed for ZTF, LUMOS's framework parallels the M4OPT toolkit for space missions, highlighting the broader applicability of MILP-based scheduling to both ground- and space-based follow-up.

Motivation: 哈勃望远镜ACS/SBC数据的背景辐射在不同观测间变化很大，特别是短波长滤光片，但背景变化的具体驱动因素尚不清楚，需要深入探究。

Here, we explore the causes of the background variation. Using over 8,000 archival SBC observations, we developed a machine learning model that can accurately predict the background for an observation, based upon a set of 23 observational parameters. This model indicates that, depending on filter, the SBC background is generally dominated by Solar elevation, Solar separation angle, Earth limb angle of observation, SBC temperature, and target Galactic latitude.
