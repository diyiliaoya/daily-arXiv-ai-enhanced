Motivation: 现有低频VLBI灵敏度不足，无法充分发挥科学潜力；SKA-LOW在低频段具有极高灵敏度，但需与其他望远镜组网才能实现高保真成像观测。因此需要构建一个包含SKA-LOW的新型低频VLBI网络。

Method: 提出在100-350 MHz频段建设低频VLBI网络，核心集成SKA-LOW，并联合亚太地区其他高灵敏度射电望远镜；优先利用现有VLBI台站进行天体测量学示范研究。

Result: 相比现有VLBA，该网络灵敏度可提升至两个数量级；结合SKA-LOW与其他望远镜可实现高保真成像，显著提升低频VLBI的科学能力。

Conclusion: 该低频VLBI网络具有巨大的科学潜力，天体测量学是验证其可行性的理想切入点，未来将推动低频VLBI科学进入新阶段。

Abstract: We propose the development of a low-frequency Very Long Baseline Interferometry (VLBI) network operating in the 100-350 MHz range, incorporating the Square Kilometre Array Low (SKA-LOW). SKA-LOW is expected to achieve exceptionally high sensitivity within this frequency band. By integrating SKA-LOW with other high-sensitivity radio telescopes located across the Asia-Pacific region, the proposed network is anticipated to deliver up to two orders of magnitude improvement in sensitivity compared to the existing VLBA. While several scientific themes utilizing low-frequency VLBI have already been proposed, we specifically advocate for astrometric studies employing existing VLBI stations to demonstrate the feasibility and scientific potential of this frequency regime. Furthermore, the combination of SKA-LOW with additional radio telescopes will enable high-fidelity imaging observations, significantly enhancing the quality and scope of low-frequency VLBI science.
